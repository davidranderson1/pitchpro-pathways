The Academy Way

Joy first. Everything grows from there.

At Lovrencsics Academy we train young players the European way: lots of touches, small-sided games, and an environment where mistakes are how you learn, not something to fear. Sessions run in Croatian and English — and the language of the game needs no translation.

For families connected through PitchPro Pathways, this is a genuine bridge into European football — the culture, the standards, and the pathway.

For Parents

What you can expect from us.

  • A family environment. The academy is small on purpose. We know every child's name, and every parent knows us.
  • Patience with every player. Children develop at their own speed. Our job is to keep them loving the game while they grow.
  • Clear communication. Croatian and English spoken. You'll always know how your child is doing and what comes next.
  • One secure system. For international families, all booking and payment runs through PitchPro Pathways — one trusted place, wherever you are.
