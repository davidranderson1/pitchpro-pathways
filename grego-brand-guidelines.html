Brand guidelines for the guide portal and PitchPro Pathways co-branded materials. Gathered from the academy's public identity (Facebook, May–July 2026). Pending: original logo file and photo library from the academy.

Identity Core

Essence

Hungarian professional roots, Croatian family academy. European football culture where every child belongs on the pitch.

Signature Line

"Every child is a champion!" — the academy's own motto. Use verbatim, never reworded.

Facts
  • Location: Split (Dračevac), Croatia
  • Ages: 2016–2019 generations
  • Languages: Croatian · English (· Hungarian)
  • Positioning: "Put profesionalne karijere" — the professional career path

Palette

Champion Green#2a8c46 · logo mark, accents
Turf#1e7d3c · cover bands, backgrounds
Badge Black#0e0f0d · logo ground, dark pages
Bright Green#3fae5a · accents on dark backgrounds
White#ffffff · wordmark, headline type
Pitch Mist#cfe8d6 · light support tint

Typography & Cover Style

Headlines — Archivo Black, uppercase
Every child is a champion
Body — Inter Regular. Warm, plain, short sentences. Bilingual where audience requires.
Signature cover treatment
White caps on turf green
The academy's cover style — bold white uppercase on textured turf green — is the signature visual. Reserved for the motto and section-divider bands.

Logo & Imagery

The Badge

Black circle · green striped-shirt footballer silhouette dribbling · "LOVRENCSICS" in white bold caps · "ACADEMY" in green letterspaced caps beneath. Keep on black or white grounds with clear space equal to the wordmark height. Production file needed from the academy.

Photography
  • Real training and match photos — kids in the green/black kit
  • Candid joy: celebrations, huddles, ball at feet
  • Stock photography
  • Staged trophy/pressure imagery

Voice

Sounds like
  • Warm, joyful, family-first
  • Proud of the European pathway, humble in tone
  • Emoji-friendly on social (👉), restrained on the portal
Never
  • Elitist selection talk ("only the best")
  • Results pressure on children
  • Guarantees of professional careers
Co-branding rule: on the PitchPro portal, the academy brand fills the page; the PitchPro top bar and footer (pitch-black + grass green) frame it. All booking and payment language points to PitchPro Pathways.
