How We Start

Understanding before training.

Most young players are told to work harder. Very few are shown what their body is actually doing. Using DARI motion capture — the same clinical-grade assessment used with professional athletes — we measure how your child moves before anyone prescribes anything.

For parents, that means two things: training built on evidence rather than opinion, and an early, honest read on injury risk — before it becomes an injury.

1

We talk first

A conversation with you and your child about goals, history, and what matters to your family. No commitment — just understanding each other.

2

We assess

A full movement screening: Motion Age, asymmetry, vulnerability, athleticism. You receive the report and a plain-language debrief.

3

We build together

A clear plan your child can understand and you can follow — whether that's sessions with me or guidance for their other coaches.

For Parents

What you can expect from me.

  • You'll always know where things stand. After every assessment you get the results explained in plain language — what we found, what it means, what we'll do about it.
  • Measured progress, not promises. Reassessment shows what changed. If something isn't working, you'll hear it from me first.
  • Your child sets the pace. The body develops on its own schedule. I don't push young athletes past what their body is ready for — that's how careers are protected, not limited.
  • One secure system. All booking, payment, and scheduling runs through PitchPro Pathways — receipts, records, and your family's data in one protected place.
