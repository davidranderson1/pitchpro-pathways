Build Plan

🤝 The Ownership Thesis (from today's meeting)

Sandro and David are aligned; the merge point is the Brain. Sandro contributes the methodology and structured knowledge; David contributes the architecture, build, and operations. Everything created around this Brain — the platform, the Guide Academy, the scoring models, the technology — is owned together. PitchPro Pathways and the practice each remain their own businesses that license/use the Brain. Exact structure (50/50 entity vs. IP agreement) to be confirmed with advice.

PHASE 1 · NOW

Capture the Knowledge

~2–4 weeks · mostly Sandro's time + Claude
  • Inventory Sandro's existing structured information (files, norms, protocols) — it's the seed of the Brain
  • Digitize into structured domains (the Knowledge Base tab of this mockup)
  • Define the Motion Age scoring spec precisely enough to compute
  • Agree the ownership structure and put it in writing
PHASE 2

Platform MVP

~4–8 weeks
  • Athlete records + assessment history (single database behind parent/guide/admin portals)
  • Scoring engine v1: capture data in → Motion Age, asymmetry, flags out
  • Wire the live portals to real data; booking + payment for the $249 screening
  • Dynamics CRM back end: reassessment triggers and follow-up pipelines
PHASE 3

The Teaching Layer

~8–12 weeks
  • Guide Academy: certification levels, modules, assessments
  • "Ask the Brain" assistant trained on the knowledge base
  • Auto-generated parent reports and executive reports
  • Executive re-norming — open the corporate market on the same engine
